SERVICES

We are committed to taking care of all your logistical requirements.

Exports - Air Freight

We have maintained an excellent working relationship with all the prime carriers operating out of India. Thus, we offer our customers some of the most competitive rates, with minimum transit times.

Exports - Sea Freight

We have a majority stake in a NVOCC to handle small non-containerized cargo, consolidate Less than Container Loads (LCL) and organize Full Container Load shipments (FCL).

Customs

We are well equipped with qualified personnel to ensure speedy clearance of goods, processing of documents and complete other statutory formalities.

IMPORTS

We offer its services for import cargo clearance for both Air & Sea shipments. Consolidations with break bulk facilities are also available.

INLAND TRANSPORT

We have our own fleet of trucks and LCVs for pick-up and deliveries in and around Mumbai. We also have a fleet of temperature-controlled trucks for the transportation of sensitive pharmaceuticals, as well as trailers fitted with gen-sets which are used for transporting reefer containers.

WAREHOUSING & PACKING

We have our own warehouse, equipped with a weighing machine and warehouse staff. At the request of our customers, we palletize their shipments so as to avoid any damage.

About

INCEPTION

Transline Air Cargo Services Pvt. Ltd. was formed in 1989 by an enterprising young man in his twenties, Dinesh Kirodian.

Since then with his hard work, dedication and vision Transline has grown by leaps and bounds. Today from it’s humble beginnings it has grown to become a well respected logistics service provider.

Over the years Transline has managed to build up an enviable list of prestigious clients whose unfailing support has helped Transline to emerge as a leading and preferred freight forwarder.

where we are now

With our head-office in Mumbai and branches in Bangalore, Chennai and Delhi we are well known in the market for the superlative and professional service we offer. With our expertise and specialisation in handling pharmaceutical shipments we have captured a niche market for ourselves.

We have kept pace with the ever changing world of logistics and the expectation of our customers expanding our scope to offer end to end logistical solutions for all.

With over 80 well trained and committed staff, we at Transline are efficiently equipped to handle all the expectations of our customers.
